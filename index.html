About Me


Greetings! My name is Dom. I was born in Philadelphia, PA on October 12th, 1990. I lived in the northwestern section of the city, Chestnut Hill, until I graduated high school in 2008. I went to college at American University in Washington, DC, where I studied International Affairs for 4 years. Always having a love for learning new languages and cultures, I studied abroad for a year in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil between 2010 and 2011.
